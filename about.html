Our mission is to remove friction from the creative process so you can focus on the music.

Our Story

SongStart began in a small bedroom studio in 2020. Frustrated by emailing MP3s back and forth and losing track of lyric versions, our founders set out to build a unified workspace for music collaboration.

Today, we help over 50,000 artists worldwide create, share, and release music together.
